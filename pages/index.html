FAQ

Support needed?

Customer support is one of our best services. We reply to all support requests within 24h, every day including Saturday and Sunday.

What is included in a Signflow license? +
A Signflow license includes lifetime access to all template files, free future updates, comprehensive documentation, and access to our support team. You can use it for one end project — either personal or for a client.
Can I use Signflow for client projects? +
Yes! Signflow can be used for client projects. Each license covers one end product. If you're building multiple client sites, you'll need a license per project, or consider our Agency plan which covers unlimited projects.
How do I get support if something isn't working? +
Our support team is available via email and live chat 7 days a week. We typically respond within 4 hours during business hours and within 24 hours on weekends. Premium plan customers get priority queue access.
Is Signflow compatible with page builders? +
Signflow is built with clean, semantic HTML/CSS/JS and is fully compatible with popular CMS platforms including WordPress, Webflow, and Shopify. We provide specific integration guides for each in our documentation.
Do you offer refunds? +
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied for any reason, contact our support team within 14 days of purchase and we'll process a full refund — no questions asked.
